About me

Hello! Thanks for visiting my website! I'm Mehrdad, your friendly neighborhood game developer! How did I end up here? Here is how...
I started to like programming after I saw my sister coding in VB.Net for her university. I was 14 when I wrote my first lines of code. After I turned 18, I decided to become a game developer. I started learning C# and Unity at university and after a month I decided to go "professional" and learn Unreal Engine! I focused on studying C++ and worked with some of my friends (CyberSoul Studio) as a gameplay programmer.
After around 2 years of mostly R&D on UE4, I decided to go back to my roots and work on my Unity skills as well. I worked as a hyper-casual game developer at FreeStateGames and prototyped several Android games.
In my spare time, I'd love to play PC games, watch TV or anime, play sports, or read memes! I'm always looking for new challenges, new things to do, and new people. Yes! I'd be glad to speak to you and share our thoughts together!
