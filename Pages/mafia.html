Mafia Nights (Online)

Online Party Game By Medrick-FZE

Overview

"Mafia Nights," developed by Medrick, is an exhilarating online party game that introduces players to the localized version of the classic game "Werewolf." Gather your friends or join random players in 10-player rooms for an engaging experience.



Technical Overview

As a game developer at Medrick, I was responsible for the continuous improvement and expansion of "Mafia Nights," a substantial project encompassing networking features, extensive UI, server communication, and more. This game utilizes cutting-edge software engineering techniques to deliver users consistent updates and fresh features regularly.
With over 50,000 daily active users, "Mafia Nights" stands as one of Iran's most significant projects, enjoying widespread success in the country's leading mobile game markets, Cafe Bazaar and Myket. Collaborating closely with designers, back-end programmers, product owners, and stakeholders, I played a vital role in charting the project's future direction.
Throughout this journey, I honed my expertise in various Unity tools, leveraging animations, profilers, analytics, and ads to enhance the game's overall performance and user experience.
"Mafia Nights" provides users with an array of engaging options, including voice-enabled gameplay, chat functionality, and a leaderboard system, fostering a sense of competition and camaraderie among players. The game's continuous evolution has been instrumental in keeping players enthralled and contributing to its ongoing success.
